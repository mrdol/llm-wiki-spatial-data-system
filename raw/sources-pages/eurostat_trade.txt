Title: Eurostat - International Trade in Goods (ITGS)

Source: Eurostat
Type: metadata / dataset description

Description:
This dataset measures international trade in goods between EU countries and non-EU countries.
It includes imports, exports, and trade balances.

Geography:
- European Union
- Euro area
- Member States

Time:
- Monthly
- Annual

Variables:
- trade value (euro)
- quantity (kg, units)
- reporting country
- partner country
- product classification (CN, SITC, BEC, CPA)
- trade flow (import/export)

Additional variables:
- mode of transport
- statistical procedure
- container (extra-EU)
- nationality of transport

Data structure:
- panel data (country x time x product)
- multi-dimensional dataset

Units:
- value: euros
- quantity: kg or specific unit

Frequency:
- monthly (short-term)
- annual (long-term)

Source system:
- Intrastat (intra-EU)
- Extrastat (extra-EU)

Data quality:
- possible missing data
- estimation for non-response
- confidentiality issues at detailed level

Key concepts:
- FOB (exports)
- CIF (imports)
- Combined Nomenclature (CN)
- SITC classification

Metadata update:
2025-10-06
