## Scope

- National French open-data portal
- Multi-producer dissemination layer rather than a single statistical producer
- Strong for open files, platform metadata, and reusable documentation

## Metadata Value

- Useful for testing metadata fields about platform quality, producer versus diffuser, resource lists, update cadence, and legal reuse conditions.
