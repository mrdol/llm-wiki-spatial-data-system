European statistical warehouse providing harmonized multi-country datasets, including labour-market, regional, and trade indicators.

## Scope

- Statistical office of the European Union
- Publishes harmonized datasets, tables, metadata pages, bulk downloads, and web services for cross-country comparison

## Relevance in This Wiki

- Important when France data must be compared consistently with other European countries
- Provides harmonized labour-force and unemployment indicators
- Provides high-value trade datasets with explicit product nomenclatures and bilateral geography
