French national statistical warehouse producing official economic, demographic, territorial, and labour-market data for France.

## Scope

- National statistical system for France
- Publishes datasets, series, methodology notes, open files, APIs, and territorial breakdowns

## Relevance in This Wiki

- Core source for France-specific unemployment, employment, and active-population series
- Likely preferred source when the research question is France-only rather than cross-country comparative
- Strong source for fine territorial metadata, including commune, Iris, and coordinate-based dissemination in some products
