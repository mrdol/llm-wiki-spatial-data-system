rotaBB: Rotavirus cases in Brandenburg, Germany, during 2002-2013 stratified by 5 age categories

Rotavirus cases in Brandenburg, Germany, during 2002-2013 stratified by
5 age categories

Description

     Monthly reported number of rotavirus infections in the federal
     state of Brandenburg stratified by five age categories (00-04,
     05-09, 10-14, 15-69, 70+) during 2002-2013.

Usage

     data(rotaBB)

Format

     A ‘sts’ object.

Source

     The data were queried on 19 Feb 2014 from the Survstat@RKI
     database of the German Robert Koch Institute
     (<https://survstat.rki.de/>).
