huddersfield: Prevalence of respiratory symptoms

Prevalence of respiratory symptoms

Description

     Prevalence of respiratory symptoms in 71 school catchment areas in
     Huddersfield, Northern England

Usage

     huddersfield

Format

     A data frame with 71 observations on the following 2 variables.

        * ‘cases’: Prevalence of at least mild conditions

        * ‘total’: Number of questionnaires returned

Source

     Martuzzi M, Elliott P (1996) Empirical Bayes estimation of small
     area prevalence of non-rare conditions, Statistics in Medicine 15,
     1867-1873, pp. 1870-1871.
