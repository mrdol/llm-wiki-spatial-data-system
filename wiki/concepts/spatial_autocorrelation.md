Dependence pattern where nearby or connected spatial units tend to have related values.

## Definition

Spatial autocorrelation means observations are not independent across space. Similar values may cluster geographically, or residuals may remain spatially structured after modeling.

## Modeling Relevance

Spatial autocorrelation can motivate spatial weights, Moran eigenvectors, spatial lag/error models, [[mgwrsar]], [[svc]], [[stvc]], or spatial validation schemes.
