# Geographical Gaussian Process GAM

A geographical Gaussian process GAM (GGP-GAM) represents spatially varying
coefficients through GAM smooths and Gaussian-process spatial structure. It is
a smooth-model alternative to kernel-based local regression approaches such as
GWR and MGWR.

## KG Use

The concept should connect papers and estimator pages where:

- spatially varying coefficients are represented using GAM smooths;
- Gaussian-process spatial terms are used for coefficient variation;
- comparisons are made against MGWR or other local regression models.
