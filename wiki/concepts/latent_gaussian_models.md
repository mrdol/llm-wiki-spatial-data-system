Latent Gaussian models represent observations through a latent Gaussian field
and hyperparameters. INLA approximates posterior marginals for this model class.

## Modeling Relevance

These models are important for areal spatial effects, SPDE spatial fields,
hierarchical models and uncertainty-aware prediction.
